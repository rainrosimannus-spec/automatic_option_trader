Margin OFF = cash-secured (notional ≤ NLV × cap, ≤1×). Margin ON = portfolio-margin proxy: per-put requirement = notional ÷ multiple, so cap admits notional up to NLV × cap × multiple (≈5× typical IBKR PM for OTM puts). Defaults mirror the live aggressive son-mode config: iv_rank_min=10, vix_halt=30, margin_cap=60%, cc_delta=0.35–0.45, cc_dte=1–7, max_positions=50, margin ON. Set IV-rank=0 and VIX-halt=100 to disable both gates for a deployment stress test. Small-account exemption: when NLV × collateral-cap is below $100k the engine skips the collateral cap entirely (mirrors live risk.py); only the margin cap constrains.
